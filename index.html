The "Ca' Sul Dos" is located on a hill overlooking Lake Garda, surrounded by a 2500 square meter olive grove and with a garden that enjoys a fantastic panoramic view of the lake and Malcesine.

THE APARTMENT

he 45 sq.m. apartment has a terrace overlooking the lake, private parking, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, linens, towels, dishwasher, washing machine, microwave, induction cooktop, Melitta coffeemaker, heating, mosquito screens on the windows, as well as deck chairs and a grill in the garden. We also make our own olive oil, which is available for purchase.

GREEN BUILDING FOR A HEALTHY VISIT

We built the house with exposed stone and other natural materials, just as it is done in the local countryside. Inside, we used mainly clay and raw earth. Both these materials are totally recyclable, environmentally friendly, naturally prevent the formation of allergy-causing molds, regulate room humidity, and have the ability to limit dust and odors in the air, thus maintaining a healthy environment. Even the wall heating is made with clay panels. For the walls, we chose stone, lime, wood, and hemp as an insulator, which also respects the modern anti-seismic requirements.
